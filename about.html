Who We Are

MediBot AI is a next-generation AI-powered healthcare assistant designed by Harshal Chatur, a B.Tech Data Science student at Raisoni Institute of Engineering, Nagpur.

Originally from Bhandara (Maharashtra, India), Harshal is a tech explorer, AI innovator, and content creator passionate about bridging healthcare & technology.

Our platform empowers people with instant AI-driven medical insights, condition awareness, and wellness support anytime, anywhere.
