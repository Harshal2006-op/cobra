Is MediBot AI a replacement for a real doctor?

No, MediBot AI is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a licensed healthcare provider for medical concerns.

How accurate is MediBot AI's medical information?

Our AI is trained on reputable medical sources. However, medicine is complex, so verify important information with your healthcare provider.

Is my health information kept private?

Yes, your data is encrypted and we comply with health data protection laws. Review our Privacy Policy for more info.

Can MediBot AI diagnose me?

MediBot AI can suggest possible conditions, but only a licensed doctor can diagnose. Use it to inform your doctor visit.

Does MediBot AI cost anything to use?

The basic version is free. Premium features like advanced tracking are available through subscription.
